# Form Template Guidelines

## Struktur Konsisten Semua Form:

1. **Toggle Header** - Preview/Edit button
2. **Preview Mode** - Tampilan sesuai TipeSoal component
3. **Edit Mode** - Form dengan spacing yang bersih

## Spacing Rules:
- Main container: `space-y-8` (tidak `space-y-6`)
- Sections: `mb-8` between major sections  
- Fields dalam section: `space-y-4`
- Grid gaps: `gap-6`

## Color Scheme:
- Container: `from-white/10 to-white/5 backdrop-blur-xl border-white/20 rounded-2xl`
- Input: `bg-white/10 border-white/20 text-white`
- Label: `text-white/80`
- Error: `border-red-400/60 bg-red-500/10 text-red-300`

## Preview Mapping:
- TipeSoal1 = Circuit Construction
- TipeSoal2 = Circuit Analysis  
- TipeSoal3 = Conceptual (Drag-drop)
- TipeSoal4 = Circuit Ordering
